A clinical team built around precise, measurable spinal correction — and around looking after you while it happens.

Clinic Owner/Director · Head Chiropractor

Dr. Richard Marchetti, DC

American Doctor of Chiropractic Advanced CBP® Certified CBP® Basic Certified ScoliBrace® Certified Sole Supports® Certified BLS & AED Provider

Dr. Richard founded CBP Precision Spine Center and leads its structural correction work as Head Chiropractor. A Parker College of Chiropractic graduate with more than 18 years of global experience in chiropractic care and scoliosis management, he holds Advanced CBP® certification — one of fewer than 200 practitioners worldwide and the first in the region. His focus is postural and structural correction for conditions such as neck pain, back pain, and spinal degeneration, always grounded in evidence-based care. Based in Muscat for over a decade, he pairs precise, measurable correction with nutritional guidance to support each patient's long-term health.
